The Craftsman

A Story of Obsession

Chef Armand Voss

Executive Chef & Founder

Born in Stellenbosch and trained under three Michelin-starred mentors across Paris, Tokyo and Copenhagen, Armand Voss returned to South Africa with one vision: to create a dining experience that felt both rooted in African terroir and alive with global technique.

His cooking philosophy is simple: find the finest ingredient at its peak, then get out of its way. Every dish at Ember begins with a fire — open flames, hot coals, smouldering wood — because Armand believes heat is the most honest expression of flavour.
