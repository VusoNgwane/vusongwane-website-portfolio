Academic Programs

Programs designed for every stage of learning

Our academic structure supports learners from foundation phase through senior levels with a focus on excellence, progression, and readiness for the future.

Foundation Phase

Building literacy, numeracy, confidence, and curiosity through structured, engaging learning experiences.

  • Language and Reading Development
  • Mathematics and Early Science
  • Creative Arts and Life Skills

Intermediate Phase

Strengthening academic depth while encouraging problem-solving, teamwork, and personal responsibility.

  • Core Academic Subjects
  • STEM Enrichment
  • Leadership and Public Speaking

Senior Phase

Preparing learners for examinations, career pathways, and confident transition into higher education or professional life.

  • Advanced Subject Streams
  • Career Guidance and Mentorship
  • Exam Preparation Support
