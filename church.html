Our Story

A Church Built on Faithful Prayer

Covenant Fire Church began in 1996 with a small group of believers gathering in Soweto for prayer, worship, and Bible study. What started in a modest setting grew over time into a vibrant church community serving families, students, professionals, and children across Johannesburg.


We are passionate about helping people know Christ, grow in faith, and walk together in biblical community. Whether you are new to church, returning after many years, or looking for a place to serve, there is room for you here.
