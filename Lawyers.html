Our Philosophy

Precision. Integrity. Results.

Founded in 1987 by Charles Hargreave SC and Adv. Nadia Morrow, our firm has grown from a boutique litigation practice into one of South Africa's most respected full-service legal institutions.

We believe that exceptional legal counsel is built on a foundation of deep expertise, relentless preparation, and an unwavering commitment to our clients' interests. Every matter, regardless of scale, receives the same rigorous attention.

Our attorneys are trusted advisors to JSE-listed corporates, multinational enterprises, government entities, and high-net-worth individuals — bringing clarity to complexity at every stage.
