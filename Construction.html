Who We Are

ENGINEERING
SOUTH AFRICA'S
FUTURE

Founded in 1998 and headquartered in Johannesburg, Ironclad Construction Group is a Grade 9 CIDB-registered contractor operating across all nine provinces. We deliver infrastructure that stands the test of time — roads, bridges, commercial complexes, industrial facilities and residential developments built with uncompromising standards.

Our workforce of 1 200 skilled tradespeople and 80 professional engineers brings precision, accountability and relentless quality to every project — regardless of scale.
